Education

We believe education is the most powerful tool for social transformation. Our education initiatives focus on:

  • Establishing and supporting rural schools
  • Digital literacy programs
  • Teacher training workshops
  • Scholarship programs for girls

Medical Care

Our healthcare initiatives bring essential medical services to underserved communities:

Our fleet of mobile clinics reaches remote villages, providing basic healthcare, medicines, and health education.

Quarterly camps with specialist doctors for eye care, dental care, and maternal health.

Workshops on nutrition, sanitation, and disease prevention in partnership with local authorities.
8 states across India 15,000+ patients treated
