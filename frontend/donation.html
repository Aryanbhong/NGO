Yes, Navi Disha Foundation is registered under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Donations made to us are eligible for 50% tax exemption under this section. You will receive a receipt for your donation that can be used when filing your taxes.

Your donation will be used to support our programs in education, healthcare, environment, and livelihood. You can specify a particular program you'd like to support during the donation process. We maintain complete transparency in our fund utilization and publish annual reports detailing how funds are spent.

Absolutely! We encourage monthly donations as they help us plan our programs more effectively. During the donation process, simply select the "Monthly" option and your chosen amount will be automatically deducted each month. You can cancel or modify your monthly donation at any time.

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, etc.), UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking from all major Indian banks, and digital wallets. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways.

You will automatically receive an email receipt immediately after your donation is processed. This receipt contains all the details needed for tax purposes. If you don't receive it, please check your spam folder or contact us at donations@navidisha.org and we'll resend it to you.
